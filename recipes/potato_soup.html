Potato Soup

Description

This is potato soup. It is traditional to eat this soup when having potatoes. Potatoes bring great luck and prosperity and slide down easily in soup form.

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put the water in a pot.
  2. Add the potatoes.
  3. Heat the water until it boils. Keep it boiling for 20 minutes.
  4. Add salt.
  5. Serve.
