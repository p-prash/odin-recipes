Tomato Soup

Description

This is tomato soup. It is traditional to eat this soup when having tomatoes. Tomatoes bring great luck and prosperity and slide down easily in soup form.

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put the water in a pot.
  2. Add the tomatoes.
  3. Heat the water until it boils. Keep it boiling for 20 minutes.
  4. Add salt.
  5. Serve.
